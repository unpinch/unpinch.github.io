Unpinch is the gesture for zooming-in - in our case into a long multi-hour episode and see the specific comments/annotations.

This gif showcases the timeline & the zoomline (which corresponds to the window on the timeline) and the comments shown below for the current window. The playback progress (cursor) is detached from the window (they are 2 separate entities) so users can go forward to look at comments without disrupting their playback flow but could rewind/fast-forward if they wish. During normal playback, if the cursor is in the zoomline and reaches the end it will auto-advance the window so that the cursor stays within the currently zoomed range and you always see the relevant comments & annotations. Comments behave the same way as in Reddit.

This is very basic/crude and the final look will be more intuitive and simpler - for example we might have a single column of comments for the current window and the zoomline could be drawn as a funnel which starts from the window above it on the timeline.
